I've written some things

The Cycle explores the complex relationship between society and menstruation, and suggests ways in which we can help overcome the burdens of menstrual-related stigmas faced by women. I seek to break down this stigma by educating readers on the many benefits of a natural menstrual cycle, including highly-effective contraception, conception assistance, maintenance of gynecological health, and timely diagnosis of many reproductive disorders.

The Cycle will teach you the details of practicing the symptothermal method of fertility awareness for these purposes, and along the way will hopefully make the taboo topic of menstruation feel a little more human. I am so passionate about this subject that I have made the book available for free here.
