A bit about me

I'm an entrepreneur, a helluva engineer, a feminist, a women's health and fertility awareness advocate, an author, a hacker-in-training, and a life-long learner.

I'm the CEO of Groove, a women's health-based startup located in Austin, Texas. I'm passionate about the intersection of women's health and technology, and I aim to make high-quality resources about female reproductive health freely available to all women. I am a strong proponent of the open source movement and I strive to align my work with that belief.

My other interests include painting, music, cycling, reading, running, traveling, and learning anything and everything I have time for. I don't believe in boredom, and I love every moment of the life I lead.

If you'd like to get in touch, I'd love to hear from you!
