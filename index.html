Howdy!

My name is Jennifer, and I live in Austin, Texas. I'm an entrepreneur, a helluva engineer, a feminist, a women's health and fertility awareness advocate, an author, a hacker-in-training, and a life-long learner.

I'm the CEO of a women's health-based startup called Groove that provides women with natural options for fertility management. I'm passionate about the intersection of women's health and technology, and I aim to make high-quality resources about female reproductive health freely available to all women. I am a strong proponent of the open source movement and I strive to align my work with that belief.

My other interests include painting, music, cycling, reading, running, traveling, and learning anything and everything I have time for.

If you'd like to get in touch, I'd love to hear from you!
