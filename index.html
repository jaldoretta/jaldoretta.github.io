Howdy!

My name is Jennifer, and I live in Austin, Texas. I'm an entrepreneur, a helluva engineer, a women's health enthusiast, a fertility awareness expert and advocate, an author, a hacker-in-training, and a life-long learner.

I'm the CEO of Groove a company that helps women take control of their reproductive health and fertility. I'm passionate about the intersection of women's health and technology, and I aim to make high-quality resources about female reproductive health freely available to all women. I am a strong proponent of the open source movement and I strive to align my work with that belief.

My other interests include painting, music, cycling, reading, running, traveling, and learning anything and everything I have time for.

If you'd like to work with me, or just say hello, get in touch! I'd love to hear from you.
