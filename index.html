Howdy!

My name is Jennifer, and I live in Austin, Texas. I’m an entrepreneur, a helluva engineer, a women’s health enthusiast, a fertility awareness expert and advocate, an author, a YouTube vlogger, a hacker, and a life-long learner.

I’m the “Co-Groover” and CEO of Groove, a company that empowers women to take control of their reproductive health and fertility by educating them about the incredible stream of data the female body creates every single day. I’m incredibly devoted to changing the conversation surrounding women’s health and breaking down social stigmas that plague women’s bodies.

Some of my other interests include painting, music, cycling, reading, running, traveling, and learning anything and everything I have time for.

If you’d like to say hello, get in touch. I’d love to hear from you!
