Howdy!

My name is Jennifer, and I live in Austin, Texas. I’m an entrepreneur, graphic designer, web developer, photographer, artist, engineer, health and fitness expert, avid biohacker, and life-long learner.

I’m the cofounder and CEO of Groove, a company that empowers women to take control of their reproductive health by educating them about the incredible stream of data the female body creates every single day.

Some of my obsessions include female reproductive health, functional fitness, the microbiome, biohacking, and building shit. I can often be spotted riding my bicycle around Austin and reading medical journals.

Check out my portfolio, and if you’re interested in working with me, get in touch. I’d love to hear from you!
